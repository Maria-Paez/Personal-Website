About Us

South Florida Immigration Protection (SFIP) is a non-profit organization dedicated to fairness and justice for immigrants in South Florida.

We provide legal support, pro-bono representation, and community education to help immigrants navigate the immigration system with dignity and confidence.

Our team works with attorneys, volunteers, and community partners to provide practical legal help, policy advocacy, and training programs for immigrants and service providers.

Our Specialties

What makes SFIP unique in its support for immigrant communities.

Legal Training

Empowering immigrants with information about work authorization, legal rights, and court preparation.

Advocacy

Supporting immigrant families through advocacy and access to safer work and community resources.

Pro-Bono Representation

Providing low-cost or free legal support to immigrants who cannot afford traditional representation.

Community Support

Building partnerships and offering resources that strengthen immigrant resilience and belonging.

What Are We Trying to Solve?

Immigrants make up roughly 20% of the workforce, yet many still lack equal access to legal support, workplace protections, and community resources.

SFIP works to close this gap by offering legal guidance, advocacy, and education so immigrants can thrive and participate fully in the local economy.
